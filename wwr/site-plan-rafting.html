Overview

Purpose

To expand the customer base further than the Potomac River and surrounding areas. Ideally allowing the online presense to expand into travel blogs and forums.

Audience

Questions:

  1. How will the audience view my website? It needs to be desktop, laptop and mobile compatible. Most people when making inital travel plans will view things from a mobile device before finalizing on a desktop or laptop.
  2. What is the Market Demographic? The target group is young to middle aged adults living near or traveling to the Potamac river near Washington DC.
  3. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Pricing, saftey features, location, adventure levels (how exciting or relaxing it seems) and availability.
